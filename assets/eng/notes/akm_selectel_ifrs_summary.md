# Selectel Financial Snapshot (IFRS/RAS 2024–H1 2025)

> **Scrape context:** AK&M release on Selectel’s interim IFRS/RAS performance used to anchor growth assumptions for the infra pitch.

## H1 2025 IFRS highlights
- **Net profit:** 1.8 B₽, matching H1 2024 while funding rapid expansion.
- **Revenue:** 8.9 B₽ (+46% YoY); core cloud infrastructure line delivered 7.7 B₽ (+47%).
- **Profitability:** Adjusted EBITDA hit 5.2 B₽ (+49%); operating expenses were 3.9 B₽.
- **CapEx:** 3.7 B₽ total with 2.5 B₽ for servers and 1.1 B₽ for new data center build‑outs (including the Yurlovsky DC slated for 2025 launch).
- **Customers:** 30.1 K active accounts, +5.3 K in 12 months, showing strong pipeline for marketplace cross-sell.

## FY 2024 baselines
- **9M 2024 IFRS:** Revenue 9.5 B₽ (+27% YoY), adjusted EBITDA 5.4 B₽ (+25%), net profit 2.7 B₽ (+16%).
- **Full-year RAS:** Revenue 13.05 B₽ (+28.1% YoY) with net profit at 2.8 B₽ (+13.8%).
- **Positioning:** Independent RU infrastructure provider with long-standing Moscow/St. Petersburg DC footprint, enabling sovereign AI workloads without foreign dependencies.

## Why it matters
- Demonstrates capacity to finance AI marketplace bets via recurring profit + bond programs.
- Validates use of 30–50% growth assumptions inside DR3 pricing scenarios.
- Highlights urgency of translating infra spend into differentiated agentic services.
